Welcome to Subway Surfers

Game Overview

Subway Surfers is an endless runner mobile game where players dash down train tracks while dodging trains and obstacles.

How to Play

  • Swipe up to jump
  • Swipe down to roll
  • Swipe left or right to change lanes

Features

  1. Colorful and vivid HD graphics
  2. Hoverboard surfing
  3. Multiple characters and power-ups
